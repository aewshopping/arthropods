Making sure that you have got all of your quills, ink bottles, quill sharpeners, spell books and sweets, you put your bag on your shoulder and leave the Great Hall to go back to the House Tower with Kensley.

Strangely enough you find you can remember the way back there easily - the path had seemed so long and winding on your first day. Perhaps it was something to do with eating the Hedgehog Egg from Percy the Picking Pigeon that has given you a homing instinct for the Tower.

When you arrive, experiencing the odd sensation of going up the tower only to emerge out from a hole in the ground, you go straight to your Hogger Hole, sit down on the bench outside your front door, and put your feet up with a happy sigh.

Refreshed you turn to Kensley who is sitting on a bench outside her hole next to you and say:
