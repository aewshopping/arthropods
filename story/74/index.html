"What did you do that for?" you cry. "I thought he was your friend!"

"Eric King is... weak," replies Azog. "His only purpose was to help me lure the chosen one - you - down here. Now that you have arrived, I no longer need him."

Azog stepped delicately over the snoring form of Professor King and advances towards you.

"Just think Arthur. Together we could achieve so much. You, the chosen one with your one-of-a-kind wand and me with all the armies of the arthropods at my command! We could do anything. We could do everything!"

While Azog is talking he is getting steadily closer and closer to you. You find yourself mesmerised by his eight tiny compound eyes.

Perhaps you should take a stand, remake the world with Azog and the insect hordes, take over the school... on the other hand if you were to stop Azog now you would be a hero and would surely win the house cup for Lionpaw.

"You must choose now Arthur," says Azog quietly. "Are you with me? Or against me?"
