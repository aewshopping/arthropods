"Let's go back to the House Tower," you say to Aaron. "I'm tired out, it must be really late now."

The two of you trudge wearily back to the Lionpaw House Tower. It is a long way but somehow you remember the path perfectly - perhaps due to some 'homing' magic that all Lionpaw's absorb when they first go to the Tower.

Before long you have climbed the stone staircase and are in the Grand Lionpaw House Vestibule - the huge open space at the base of the tower, lit up with beautiful magical light-globes, and marble floor polished to a sheen by giant enchanted snails, and a faint smell of chlorine from the water park. You feel your spirits lift: could anyone have a better home to come back to than this?

"Night night Arthur," says Aaron with a yawn. "See you in the morning."

You both wearily climb the final stone staircase to your sleeping apartments, and enter.

Throwing your robes over the sofa, you have a quick refreshing shower in the ensuite bathroom, dry yourself with an enormous fluffy white towel, tuck yourself up into your luxurious four-poster bed and drift off to sleep.
