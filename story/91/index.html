With a groan you open your eyes.

Looking around you you appear to be in the Hogwarts School hospital. You see Daniel lying next to you on an identical bed, also groaning. Various medical instruments hang on the walls: stethoscopes, ear trumpets, bone-saws, syringes and thaumometers.

How did I get here? you wonder. Then it comes back to you and you shudder: the dungeon.

Something bad is definitely going on down there. Now even more than before you realise that you have to find out whatever it is that is happening down there, figure out what is going on and put a stop to it. It is up to you - and for next time you know at least one choice that you should not make again...

"Hello Arther and Daniel. I see you have returned to the conscious realm." Madame Blavatsky, the Professor of Defence Against Dark Magic walks into the room.

"Wssrrflbittit?" you mumble.

"Yes you had quite a shock back there I'm sure. When Slumblebore found you, you were both completely unconscious. But don't worry you will make a full recovery - the antiodarkotics I gave you will flush all the bad magic out shortly."

"Thank you" you manage to say.

"But the most important thing is that you get some rest. I'm going to cast a sleeping spell on you now, timed so that you will wake up tomorrow morning at 7am. When you are sleeping soundly I will teleport you to your own bedrooms in your House Tower."

As she chants the magic words "somnolencia salchicha sabrosa" you try to speak but you can't stop yourself drifting away.

What is in the dungeon? you desperately want to ask. Your lips form the word "Wha...?"...
