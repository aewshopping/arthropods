Tweetle beetle bottle battle!" you cry as you round the corner with Snako just behind you.

Aiming your wand at the beetles... nothing happens.

"What are you trying to do?" shouts Snako.

"I don't know! I just thought that might work!"

"That's not even magic that's just gibberish!"

"Well I don't see you doing any better, why don't you try to..."

But you never get the chance to tell Snako what he should try to do. Three pops are followed by three blasts of superheated acid.

You try to duck out of the way but you are just a fraction too slow. One splurge of acid grazes your forehead and you fall to the ground, overcome.

Snako hits the ground heavily beside you.

The last thing you see before you lose consciousness is the three giant bombardier beetles doing what appears to be a celebratory dance at the far end of the corridor.
