"Tonto tijeras trae tiempo!" you shout out, waving your wand above your head.

To your surprise, a golden pair of magical scissors shoots out of the end of your wand and strikes the ceiling, and golden threads arch through the stone above your head.

From the golden filaments in the roof, a flutter of beautiful golden snowflakes fall. Each one has an exquisitely perfect hexagonal symmetry.

When they land on your arms and head, they give you a warm tingling sensation and then disappear with a tinkling sound, like a tiny bell.

After a few moments the golden snow fall stops and the corridor returns to its previous gloomy state.
