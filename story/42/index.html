You find yourself in a magical place that exists outside time and outside space.

The only way to reach here is by powerful magic. No path can take you here. No decision you make will lead you to this place.

Only the very best wizards can command this level of magical power.

You appear to be floating in a sort of void. If you want to move you only have to think yourself moving. Across this void glimmer infinitely long twists and curtains of shimmering golden sparkles: raw magic.

It is peaceful. You feel relaxed and calm. The troubles of the world do not reach here.

You think yourself over to a golden pillar of glittering magical dust and let it run over your hand. You feel the magic enter your veins and your magical strength increases further.

Now that you have found this place and touched the magic dust you only have to think of the magic number "42" and you will be instantly teleported back here.

Looking around you notice transparent rainbow bubbles floating between the twisting columns of golden dust.

You look closer.

Each bubble is the size of a football. Inside the bubbles you can see figures moving - people. You recognise yourself in the bubbles.

You realise that the bubbles must be windows into your past - and your future. Each bubble is an important moment in your life. As soon as you realise this you also know that all you have to do is reach into a bubble and you will be transported to that time and place.

A feeling of deep happiness settles upon you. You can go to any back - or forwards - to anytime in your life it is your choice.

Or should you double your efforts, try two times as much as you are now, and explore this magical void a little more?

But anytime you want you can come back here, to this shimmering golden magical void, filled with peace - just by thinking the magic number: 42.

Will you reach into a bubble - or linger here a while longer?
