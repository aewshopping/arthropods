"Vamos varita vencer vistosamente!" you exclaim as dramatically as you can manage.

A flickering golden light slowly extends from the top of your wand until it is about a metre long. You swing the "light sword" around and it makes a satisfying swishing zooming sound. Your whole body thrills with the sensation of power.

"Aaah yes!" you hiss, and swipe the nearest strand of spider silk. Despite it being as thick as a steel cable and twice as tough, the light sword slices through it with no effort at all and the spider web flops to the floor.

You steadily walk forwards, cutting through the spider web with your magical golden light sword, feeling the magic from the wand course through your body. The sensation of this powerful magic is delicious.

Soon the spider web has gone. You and Snako race down the passageway and skid around a corner...
